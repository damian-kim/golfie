# Product

## Register

product

## Users
Golfers and software engineers who want to analyze practice shot parameters (launch angle, speed, carry) from dual-device camera feeds. They require clear, immediate feedback under active training conditions without distracting UI elements.

## Product Purpose
Perform high-speed video synchronization, 2D ball tracking, 3D stereo triangulation, and physics-based trajectory reconstruction. Visually replay the reconstructed flight paths in a 3D driving range environment.

## Brand Personality
Minimal, flat, and quiet. The interface is precise, focused, and understated, presenting technical data without visual clutter or shouting.

## Anti-references
Cluttered consumer arcade-style layouts and loud, overstimulating interfaces that compete for the user's attention.

## Design Principles
1. **Data over Noise:** Focus on clarity and precision. Visual hierarchy should highlight raw numbers and trajectory curves before decorative accents.
2. **Scientific Honesty:** Distinguish clearly between measured raw data, estimated parameters, and fitted trajectory paths to prevent false confidence.
3. **Unobtrusive Interface:** Controls and details should recede into the background, leaving the 3D shot viewer clear and readable.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- Target WCAG AA color contrast ratios for text.
- Provide keyboard accessibility for all video playback and replay triggers.
- Support `prefers-reduced-motion` media queries by disabling camera sweep transitions and utilizing clean jump-cuts.
